Character Creation

Created by Deer Devil

 

  Making a character can be difficult, or at least unthinkable. You might think that I'm going to teach you to draw, sorry but no. This is about before the drawing and thinking "How will I imagine this character" first off, this character is about you. Think about all the things that make you, you. Like your favorite color and/or animal. Mine is a Deer and my favorite color is Pink, so I made my character named Deer Devil. Similar to the word Dare Devil since I like to do dangerous things (sometimes). Don't worry you can fudge one thing and one thing only. This is about you, yourself, and you personally. Take a few minutes to think of a name and what your character would look like. If you can draw, then create what makes your heart at best. You think you're done? You still have a few steps, like props, fears, and things that would make your character handy. Deer Devil's prop is a clothes hanger and scissors, and yet her greatest fear is getting cut. You see? That's what makes her so unique in the character world. If you've thought it through and are happy about then I hope I was helpful.
